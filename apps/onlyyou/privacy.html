OnlyYou 隐私政策

Effective Date / 生效日期：2026-07-18

English

1. Introduction

OnlyYou is an Android application that helps you manage your phone's Do Not Disturb mode. You can set up a whitelist of phone numbers; while Do Not Disturb mode is enabled, calls from whitelisted numbers will still alert you, ensuring that you never miss important calls.

2. We Do Not Collect Any Data

OnlyYou does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app has no account system, and all features are available without registration or sign-in. All of your data (including whitelisted phone numbers) is kept solely on your device. We do not access, upload, or share this data in any way.

3. Permissions

To provide its core functionality, OnlyYou requests only the following two permissions:

  • Do Not Disturb Access: used solely to turn your phone's Do Not Disturb mode on or off according to your settings.
  • Read Contacts: used solely to let you choose whitelisted phone numbers from your contacts. The selected numbers are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

OnlyYou does not request or use any other system permissions.

4. Third-Party Services

OnlyYou does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or statistics SDKs, and does not share your information with any third party. The app does not communicate with any third-party servers while it is running.

5. Data Security

Because OnlyYou performs no data transmission, your data remains entirely under the control of your device. All information, including your whitelist, is stored locally on your device only. When you uninstall the app, the associated data is removed by the system together with the app.

6. Children's Privacy

OnlyYou is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. As the app does not collect data from any user, no data collection takes place when children use the app either.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed of the latest version. Your continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us at:

vaycentsun@gmail.com
